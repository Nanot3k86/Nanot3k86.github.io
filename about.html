Hello, I'm Nate Barrere, born and raised in the rugged landscapes of Montana. My journey into the realm of design began at the age of 14, when I embarked on my first job as an apprentice to a seasoned graphics designer. Under their mentorship, I expanded my creative passion for art into the world of Photoshop, vector art, product photography, and layout design. Since then, I've spent my free time becoming fluent in the industry’s most advanced software and taking on freelance work in design and illustration. Alongside my professional pursuits, I pursued higher education, wrapping up a bachelor’s degree in media arts, design, marketing, and communications from SNHU. I'm passionate about leveraging design to make meaningful connections and bring ideas to life in unique fashion.

Thanks for stopping by and don’t forget to check out my portfolio section for recent works, and my contact section to stay in touch.
